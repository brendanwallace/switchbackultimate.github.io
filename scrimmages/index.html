Open Scrimmages

Every other Saturday (not counting tournaments) during the season we host a scrimmage for interested players to come get reps with players on our team.

Follow this link and then click “join group” at the top to receive emails about where and when these are happening: https://groups.google.com/g/switchback-scrims/

Scrimmages are on hold since we are gearing up for the end of the season. We hope to have some postseason scrimmages coming up later this Fall, stay posted!
