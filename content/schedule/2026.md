# 2026 Schedule


## Practice

We practice Wednesday nights and Sunday mornings. Later in the summer, we may drop Wednesday nights in favor of a Saturday practice.

We also plan to hold 1-2 double practice weekends during the season.

## Tournaments

Tournaments are still being finalized, but we plan to attend 3 tournaments and the series this year.

## Phases

We structure our season in three phases:

- Phase I (June-July): We practice a lot, learn our systems, and grow closer as a team.
- Phase II (end of July-early August): We offload and let people get a break, travel, remember that they sometimes have other hobbies and friends, etc.
- Phase III (mid-August through the series): We ramp back up and aim to reach our competitive peak for the series.
